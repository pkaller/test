Regulated Investment Programs

Regulated fund structures
for digital assets.

01

Token Principal Programme

Designed for project founders and early insiders managing concentrated token positions, this structure allows Mosaic Capital to implement a systematic management program for participating holders. By managing holdings collectively under a regulated fund, founders avoid the MNPI and coordination challenges that come with individual discretionary trading — while still benefiting from systematic income generation and disciplined trading.

SEC RegulatedMNPI ManagedManaged Trading
02

Single-Asset Yield Products

Dedicated fund products for investors seeking yield on a specific cryptocurrency. Mosaic Capital manages each portfolio as asset manager, employing lending, credit, and yield-generation strategies tailored to that asset. Each product is isolated within its own segregated portfolio, keeping exposures clean and strategies focused. Suited to institutions and family offices that hold a core position and want a regulated, managed approach to generating returns on it.
