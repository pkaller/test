Execution

How we trade:
The RFQ process.

Every options trade is executed via Request for Quote — the institutional standard that ensures best pricing, documented audit trails, and full transparency on every fill.

01

Strategy Selection

Strike, expiry, and size determined by client IPS and income objectives.

02

RFQ Broadcast

Request sent simultaneously to all leading market makers — ensuring competitive quotes even for smaller or less liquid tokens.

03

Quote Competition

3–8 dealers compete, driving tighter spreads than open-market execution. All quotes time-stamped.

04

Best Execution Fill

Best quote selected and executed. Full record of competing quotes retained for audit.

05

Post-Trade Report

Same-day position report for every trade: strike, expiry, premium received, and P&L impact.
