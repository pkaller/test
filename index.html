Services

Investment advisory
and asset management.

Mosaic Capital provides investment advisory and asset management services to qualifying institutional clients. Our services include portfolio management, lending and yield, spot trading, and governance advisory — each delivered within Mosaic Capital's broader investment advisory framework.

01

Foundation Asset Management

End-to-end portfolio management for crypto foundations — Investment Policy Statement design, multi-asset allocation, liquidity planning, and quarterly board reporting.

02

Digital Asset Management

Discretionary management of digital asset portfolios — including U.S. Treasury assets, high-yield private credit, and DeFi yield — within segregated structures designed to be bankruptcy-remote, subject to the relevant offering documents.

03

Lending & Yield

Institutional lending programs that seek to generate yield on idle assets — counterparty lending against collateral, DeFi yield across audited protocols, and structured credit facilities — all under documented risk-management procedures with real-time exposure monitoring.

04

Spot Trading & Execution

Efficient-execution spot trading desk for foundations and institutions. VWAP, TWAP, and competitive RFQ across multiple venues — with documented post-trade reporting on every fill.

05

Governance Advisory

Board education, Investment Policy Statement review, and fiduciary training for foundation trustees and investment committees.

Regulatory Status

Mosaic Capital Inc. is incorporated in the Cayman Islands and registered with the Cayman Islands Monetary Authority (“CIMA”) as a Registered Person under the Securities Investment Business Act (Revised). Mosaic Capital Inc. is also registered as an Investment Adviser with the U.S. Securities and Exchange Commission (“SEC”). Principal place of business: Cayman Islands.

Mosaic Capital Inc. is not registered with the U.S. Commodity Futures Trading Commission (“CFTC”). Mosaic Capital relies on the exemption from registration as a commodity trading advisor available under CFTC Rule 4.14(a)(8)(i)(D). Pools managed by Mosaic Capital, where applicable, operate in reliance on the exemption from registration as a commodity pool operator available under CFTC Rule 4.13(a)(3). Pursuant to these exemptions, Mosaic Capital is not required to deliver a CFTC disclosure document to pool participants and is subject to a different regulatory framework than registered commodity pool operators and commodity trading advisors. Pool interests are offered exclusively pursuant to a confidential offering memorandum on a private placement basis.
